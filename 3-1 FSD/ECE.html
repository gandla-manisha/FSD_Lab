The department of Electronics and Communications Engineering was established in the year 1997 with an intake of 60 students for an Under-graduate program. The intake was enhanced to 180 students in the year 2012. The department also offers a post-graduate program – M.Tech. in Digital Electronics and Communication Engineering stream. The UG course was accredited twice by National Board of Accreditation for 3 years in 2017 & 2021, NAAC for 5 years in 2018 and UGC AUTONOMOUS for 10 years in 2018.

The department of ECE is headed by Dr. K.Ragini, a compelling personality with a progressive vision, who has 26 years of vast teaching experience. The department has 39 dedicated and experienced teaching faculty with 10 Doctorates, 3 faculty have submitted their Ph.D.thesis, 15 are pursuing Ph.D. at reputed universities – IITKGP, IIITH, NITW, OU and KLU. Department has well qualified 10 non-teaching staff. Faculty have received 17 Awards which includes Best Teacher Award, Best Researcher Award, NPTEL Discipline Award and Best IETE student forum (ISF) coordinator Award for consecutive 2 years.

The department has sixteen established laboratories – Python Programming, Computer Networks, Basic Simulation, Microprocessors & Microcontrollers, ARM MPDSP(PG), Electronic Devices & Circuits, Analog Circuits, Digital Electronics & Logic Design, Analog & Digital Communications, Digital Signal Processing, Advanced Wireless Communication(PG,) e-CAD AND VLSI LAB, Digital System Design Lab (PG), Electronic Design Lab, Microwave Engineering Lab, Advanced Communication Networks Lab (PG) which are fully equipped with test & measuring equipment and trainers specially designed for the practical work. A total of 11 classrooms with a strength of 80 students for each class are provided in the department. The department is also equipped with an ultra-modern digital E-classroom equipped with 80 computer systems, smart board for interactive teaching.

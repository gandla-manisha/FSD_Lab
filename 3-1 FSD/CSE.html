The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 1999 with an intake of 30 for Under Graduate Program and enhanced to 90 in 2002, 120 in 2007 and from 2013 the intake is 180. In 2007, with an intake of 18, Post Graduate program was started. The UG course was accredited by National Board of Accreditation for 3 years in 2017, NAAC for 5 years in 2018 and UGC AUTONOMOUS for 10 years in 2018.

The Department of CSE is expertly headed by Dr. A. Sharada, an eminent and dynamic academician who has an extensive experience of 26 years which includes 8 years of proven track record Industrial experience. The department has competent and hardworking faculty with 37 in number (9 Doctorates, 4 Associate Professor and 28 Assistant Professors associated with the additional support of non-teaching and technical staff).

Launch of 2 Centre of Excellence (CoE) – IoT & Analytics Center of Excellence and AI & ML Center of Excellence and 2 Incubation Centres – Bookmystall and Intrinsic Science Labs in the department has been made a big asset for faculty and students to explore and extend their knowledge in current emerging trends and find their career opportunities. The CoE & Incubation centers are equipped with latest kits and devices namely, JETSON NANO, NVIDIA JETSON TX2 DEVELOPER KIT, INTEL REAL SENSE CAMERA and many other latest highly configured kits, where the faculty and students can fetch a great advantage in processing their research in a smoother way.
